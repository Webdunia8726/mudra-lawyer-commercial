Who We Are

Mudra Realty is a prominent commercial real estate consultancy firm specializing in Commercial Leasing and associated services. The company primarily operates in the State Capital Region of Uttar Pradesh, i.e., Lucknow.

We fill the vacuum in high-end commercial leasing space, catering to ₹5 lacs a month and above. Under the umbrella of Mudra Management Companies Pvt. Ltd. and the vision of co-founder Mandeep Singh, we ventured into the realty business back in 2011.

With growing demand, Mudra Realty now offers end-to-end commercial leasing management in Lucknow, committed to excellence and growth.
